Application Security architect | Tel Aviv

Bancor is looking for a professional Application Security Architect to implement and carry out various processes and best practices. In this position you will be asked to establish industry-leading security processes and practices at each phase of the software development lifecycle. You will provide guidance on application security design and architecture, coordinate the prioritization of critical security-related activities and organize educational initiatives and materials. The successful candidate will be a part of the R&D department and will report to the VP of R&D.

Responsibilities

  • Lay out the security architecture and operational roadmap for the R&D and Product departments.
  • Participate in strategic activities to communicate security objectives and ensure their appropriate consideration in product and operational planning.
  • Research enterprise security and privacy standards and best-practices and ensure proper and accurate implementation.
  • Participate in functional and technical initiation activities to incorporate effective threat modeling and security standards and best practices into product design.
  • Implement different aspects of application security issues, in CI and CD processes.
  • Guiding participants in code reviews.
  • Create an external (read: public facing) reporting process for security and stability related reports and manage artifacts lifecycle through the development process.
  • Assist in our vulnerability remediation efforts by establishing effective triaging of bug findings and security scans, coordinating engineering response and guiding teams through the implementation of fixes.
  • Establish and direct post-breach recovery procedures.
  • Provide regular updates to department and company leadership on our platform’s security posture. Ensure cross-department collaboration and coordination of security efforts.
  • Offer plans and solutions for different security scenarios.

Requirements

  • Offensive-Security mindset, and the passion to instill it into other engineers.
  • In-depth experience identifying and protecting against web application and web service security vulnerabilities.
  • Detailed technical knowledge of techniques, standards and state-of-the art capabilities for authentication and authorization, security vulnerabilities and remediation.
  • Familiarity with security auditing tools and frameworks.
  • Experience in bug-bounty processes.
  • Knowledgeable about tactics, techniques, and procedures used for software security exploitation.
  • Highly motivated, able to define a vision and lead its execution, driven to overcome obstacles.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills. Ability to clearly articulate specifications and best practices for application security.
  • B.S. / M.S. in Computer Science.
  • 5+ years work experience in an application security role.
  • Prior enterprise software engineering experience.
  • Strong understanding of software security architecture and cloud platforms.
